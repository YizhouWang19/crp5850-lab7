Public and Affordable Housing Flood Risk

Description:This map is linked to how extreme weather affects shoreline properties in New York City. Also, this map illustrates the distribution of affordable housing, therefore, this map will illustrate how vulnerable groups are disproportionately impacted by climate change.

The data source is from Sandy's inundation zone data in blue and New York City Housing Developments (NYCHA) from NYC OpenData in purple to illustrate the overlaps.
